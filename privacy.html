Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Transparency Reports Android app (the “App”).

Data We Collect

None. The App does not collect personal data, device identifiers, usage analytics, or crash reports. No account or login is required to use the App.

Data We Fetch

The App fetches publicly available transparency datasets from kieranmaynard.com — the same data that powers the web dashboards on this site. These are government and platform transparency reports drawn from public sources (Google Transparency Report, EU DSA Database). No request payload contains personal information.

Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the App.

Data Retention

Fetched data is held in memory for the duration of the session only and is not written to device storage.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
