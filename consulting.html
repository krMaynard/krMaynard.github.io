Consulting Services

I'm not loyal to any tool. I'm an expert at getting maximum value out of whatever the best one is right now — earlier than the people around me, and in production, not in theory.

That's a pattern, and it's an old one. In 2009 the leverage was in spaced-repetition software and OCR; I used them to teach myself Japanese and Chinese to professional fluency, years before language apps were mainstream. When the leverage moved to SQL and data pipelines, I cut a 60–80 person-hour compliance reporting cycle to under 5 hours. When it was browser automation, I saved $50k+ in vendor time with tooling that processed tens of thousands of cases. The throughline across 11+ years at Google and Roblox, and years before that as a self-taught linguist, is the same: find where the real leverage has moved, get fluent fast, and turn it into a reliable system before it's obvious.

Right now that frontier is agentic AI. My current work is getting the most out of Claude Code — wiring coding agents into real workflows so they do meaningful work reliably, not just demos.

If you want someone who'll find the highest-leverage tool for your problem and actually make it pay off, reach out at kieranmaynard{at}gmail{dot}com.

Where I help most

  • Agentic AI in real workflows: putting coding agents (Claude Code, Codex, Google Antigravity) to work on actual production tasks — scoping what they're good for, building the guardrails, and measuring whether they're really saving time.
  • Process audits: finding the manual bottleneck that's quietly costing the most, and designing the replacement around today's best tooling.
  • AI for trust & safety and compliance: applying AI and automation to transparency reporting and regulatory obligations (DSA, CMA, AB 587) — turning them into product requirements and the systems that deliver them.
