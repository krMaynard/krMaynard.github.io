Roblox's California and New York Transparency Reports Are Live

April 1, 2026
Roblox Compliance PM Transparency Regulatory

Two state social media transparency reports are now filed — Roblox's California AB 587 (H2 2025) and New York S895 (Q4 2025).

The two statutes overlap in scope but diverge on specifics. AB 587 focuses on content moderation policies and their enforcement — what the policies say, what detection methods are used, aggregate enforcement figures by category. S895 requires more granular operational data: counts of specific actions taken against content and accounts, broken down by category, on a quarterly basis. Running both filings in parallel meant maintaining separate data tracks while sharing cross-functional review capacity across Legal, Policy, and Engineering — a useful forcing function for building more durable reporting infrastructure.

A separate post covers what distinguishes AB 587 from S895 in more detail — including the infrastructure implications and where the two statutes require different data pipelines even when review cycles can be shared.
