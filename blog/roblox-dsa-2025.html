Roblox's EU Digital Services Act Transparency Report (2025) Is Live

March 1, 2026
Roblox EU DSA Compliance PM Transparency

Roblox has published its EU Digital Services Act transparency report for 2025. The report covers Roblox's content moderation activity for 1 January – 31 December 2025, including notices received and actions taken across EU services. Alongside it, Roblox also published its Terrorist Content Online (TCO) transparency report for the same period.

I owned end-to-end delivery: scoping data requirements against Article 15 obligations, building the cross-functional production workflow across Legal, Policy, and Engineering, and driving on-time publication.

Roblox is not a VLOP, so the report is an annual filing under Article 15 rather than the semi-annual requirement under Article 42. The practical difference — annual cadence, no bi-annual breakdown requirement — shapes the production workflow considerably. The Article 15 data requirements are still non-trivial, particularly the breakdown of own-initiative moderation actions by content category and the documentation of automated tool use, but the timeline and data shape are more manageable than the Article 42 VLOP obligations.

A separate post covers what EU DSA transparency reporting actually requires for both Article 15 platforms and VLOPs — including what Article 42 adds and what that means for reporting infrastructure.
